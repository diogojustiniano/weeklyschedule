Training reference

What each session type actually means
Z2 / Easy Run
Conversational · heart rate 130–145
You should be able to hold a full conversation. If you can't, slow down. 80% of weekly volume. Ego-free running. Pace roughly 7:00–7:30/km.
Tempo @ Marathon Pace
From week 13 · comfortably hard
Your goal marathon pace is ~6:25/km (for 4:30 finish). Warm up 2km, hold MP, cool 2km. Teaches body to hold pace when tired.
Intervals
400m / 800m / 1km reps
Run faster than 10K pace (target ~5:30/km). Equal-time jog recovery. Builds VO₂max and running economy. Hurt on purpose, not by accident.
The Long Run
Saturday · the whole point
Slower than Z2 is fine. Finishing strong is the only metric. Practice fueling from 90 min onward (gels, water). Marathon won or lost here.
Strength / Lift
3×/week · 45 min
Compound focus: squat, deadlift, bench, row, overhead press. Drop intensity from week 20. Lifting is servant of the run, not the master.
Cutback Week
Weeks 4, 8, 12, 16, 20 · 30–40% volume drop
Non-negotiable. Adaptation happens in recovery, not in work. Skip cutbacks and you pick up an injury week 15 and lose the race.
